# pocketlinks-privacy
PocketLinks Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 27, 2026

PocketLinks is built and maintained by an independent developer. Your privacy is important, and PocketLinks is designed to collect as little information as possible.

Information Collection

PocketLinks does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.
Specifically:
No user accounts are required
No personal information is requested
No analytics or tracking frameworks are used
No data is sent to external servers
All bookmarks and folders created in PocketLinks are stored locally on your device.

Bookmark Data

Any links, titles, folders, or browser preferences you save in PocketLinks remain on your device unless you choose to back them up using iOS system features such as iCloud.
PocketLinks does not have access to this data.

Third-Party Services

PocketLinks does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.
If you choose to open a bookmark, you will be redirected to your selected web browser or app. Any data handling that occurs after that point is governed by the privacy policies of those third-party apps or websites.
